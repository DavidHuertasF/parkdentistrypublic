Park Dentistry was born because we want to show that it is possible to have a good time in a doctor's practice.

It may sound impossible but we want to achieve it by showing the positive side of health!

By implementing a different concept of doctor, patient and practice through experience.

Experience that in the long term will build habits that guarantee prevention and awareness of a comprehensive health that begins in the mouth

We provide a close, welcoming, comfortable, professional and fun service, for every mom, dad and child.

We have a waiting room for parents to be confortable managing different activities and kids playing around just befores their appointment.
